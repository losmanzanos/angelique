Therapeutic Philosophy

Approach

A space to slow down and reconnect

Therapy here is depth-oriented and insight-focused — drawing on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Client-Centered, and Mindfulness-Based approaches to meet you where you are. Together, we slow things down, untangle what feels overwhelming, and create space for clarity and emotional steadiness.

Whether you’re working through relationship challenges, redefining your sense of self, or trying to reconnect with your partner, the work moves at a pace that feels right — without the pressure of having everything figured out before you begin.

How I Work

Guiding Principles

Your Story Matters

Your experiences, feelings, and perspectives are at the center of this work. There’s no judgment — only genuine curiosity about who you are and what you’re navigating.

Collaborative Process

Therapy is a partnership. We work together to identify what’s keeping you stuck and experiment with new ways of thinking and being that feel authentic to you.

Practical & Meaningful

While we explore the deeper dimensions of your experience, we also focus on concrete tools and strategies you can use in your daily life right away.
