Therapeutic Philosophy

How I Practice

A space to slow down and reconnect

My work draws on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Client-Centered, and Mindfulness-Based approaches — not as a script to follow, but as a set of lenses to apply where each one fits. Some of the work is structural: examining the thoughts, beliefs, and patterns that quietly shape how you move through the day. Some of it is more spacious: making room for grief, anger, longing, or doubt that doesn’t have a tidy solution.

What stays constant is the pace. Therapy here moves at the speed of you, not the speed of a treatment plan. You don’t need to arrive with insight, a goal, or the right words — only a willingness to sit with what’s hard and see what shifts.

Guiding Principles

Your Story Matters

Your experiences, feelings, and perspectives are at the center of this work. There’s no judgment — only genuine curiosity about who you are and what you’re navigating.

Collaborative Process

Therapy is a partnership. We work together to identify what’s keeping you stuck and experiment with new ways of thinking and being that feel authentic to you.

Practical & Meaningful

While we explore the deeper dimensions of your experience, we also focus on concrete tools and strategies you can use in your daily life right away.
