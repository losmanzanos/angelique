A Place to Begin

Individual Therapy

A space created for you

Individual therapy is a space created just for you — where your story is heard, your experiences are honored, and your growth is supported with intention and care. Whether you're navigating impactful life transitions, loss of identity, relationship challenges, or simply feeling stuck, therapy offers an opportunity to slow down, reflect, and reconnect with yourself in a meaningful way.

We begin with your story — how you got here, what matters most, and what tends to stand in your way. Tools, frameworks, and practices come after, shaped around the specifics of your life rather than handed down from a manual.

Couples Therapy

Rebuilding connection

Through couples sessions, we'll explore the patterns, emotions, and experiences shaping your relationship, while building practical tools to help you move forward with clarity and confidence as a connected unit. Therapy isn't about "fixing" you — it's about understanding each other, strengthening your connection, and creating lasting change at a pace that feels right collectively.

A space where both partners' experiences and perspectives are heard with equal weight — whether you're facing specific conflicts, feeling disconnected, navigating infidelity, or simply wanting to deepen your bond.

Group Therapy

Connection through community

Group therapy offers a powerful and often deeply healing experience — reminding you that you don't have to navigate life's challenges alone. In a supportive and thoughtfully guided space, you'll connect with others who share similar experiences, fostering a sense of understanding, validation, and community that can be difficult to find elsewhere.

Each group has its own focus and pace, with strict confidentiality as the foundation. Offerings vary seasonally; the Support Groups page covers what’s currently running and how to inquire about a fit.

Reproductive Support

Intended Parent & Surrogate Assessments

The journey through third-party reproduction — whether you’re an Intended Parent (IP) pursuing surrogacy or a Gestational Carrier offering this extraordinary gift — carries real emotional complexity. Angelique provides clinical psychological evaluations and assessments required by fertility clinics and reproductive agencies, as well as ongoing mental health support throughout every stage of the process.

For Intended Parents (IPs): Psychological evaluations assessing emotional preparedness and readiness for surrogacy, along with supportive counseling as you navigate fertility treatments, donor decisions, and the waiting that comes with it.

For Gestational Carriers & Surrogates: Clinical assessments that meet the screening requirements of agencies and fertility clinics, plus ongoing support to protect your wellbeing throughout the surrogacy journey.

This is an actively growing area of Angelique’s practice. Reach out to discuss availability, what the assessment process involves, and how ongoing support can be structured around your specific situation.
