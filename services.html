Ways We Can Work Together

Individual Therapy

A space created for you

A dedicated space where your story is heard, your experiences are honored, and your growth is supported with intention and care. For those navigating impactful life transitions, loss of identity, relationship challenges, or simply feeling stuck.

The foundation of this work is understanding you — your history, your values, what matters most to you, and what stands in your way. From there, we build tools tailored to your life, moving at a pace that feels manageable and sustainable.

$175 per 50-minute session

Couples Therapy

Rebuilding connection

A space where both partners’ experiences and perspectives are heard with equal weight. Whether you’re facing specific conflicts, feeling disconnected, navigating infidelity, or simply wanting to deepen your connection, this work helps you understand each other more fully.

We explore the patterns that keep you stuck, the emotions beneath the surface, and the needs that go unmet. Together, we build practical communication tools and create space for genuine understanding.

$190 per 50-minute session

Group Therapy

Connection through community

A reminder that you don’t have to navigate life’s challenges alone. In a supportive, thoughtfully guided space, you’ll connect with others who share similar experiences — fostering understanding, validation, and community that can be difficult to find elsewhere.

Groups are kept intentionally small — 6-8 members — to ensure there’s adequate time and space for everyone to be heard and supported. All groups maintain strict confidentiality.
