Approach

A space to slow down and reconnect

Therapy here is depth-oriented and insight-focused — drawing on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Client-Centered, and Mindfulness-Based approaches to meet you where you are. Together, we slow things down, untangle what feels overwhelming, and create space for clarity and emotional steadiness.

Whether you’re working through relationship challenges, redefining your sense of self, or trying to reconnect with your partner, the work moves at a pace that feels right — without the pressure of having everything figured out before you begin.

Services

Ways we can work together

Individual Therapy

A space created just for you — where your story is heard, your experiences are honored, and your growth is supported with intention and care. For those navigating impactful life transitions, loss of identity, relationship challenges, or simply feeling stuck.

Couples Therapy

We explore the patterns, emotions, and experiences shaping your relationship while building practical tools to move forward with clarity and confidence as a connected unit. The aim isn’t to “fix” anyone — it’s to understand each other and create lasting change at a pace that feels right.

Group Therapy

A reminder that you don’t have to navigate life’s challenges alone. In a supportive, thoughtfully guided space, you’ll connect with others who share similar experiences — fostering understanding, validation, and community that can be difficult to find elsewhere.

Clinician

Angelique Danza, M.S., LMFT

Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist based in Murrieta, California, with over a decade of experience supporting children, adolescents, adults, couples, and groups. Drawn to understanding people — their stories, their struggles, and the ways they find strength even in the most difficult seasons of life.

Clinical experience spans grief and loss, anger management, relational conflict, life transitions, identity loss, anxiety and depression, foster home dynamics, women’s mental health, and reentry work with PC 290 registrants focused on reducing recidivism and supporting public safety.
