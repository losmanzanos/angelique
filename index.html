Welcome

You’ve found your way here

Reaching out for therapy takes extreme self awareness. Whether you’re moving through a transition, untangling a relationship, or simply trying to feel like yourself again, you don’t have to make sense of it before getting in touch.

At Pivotal Tides, we believe that everyone has the ability to lead a happy and fulfilling life. Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that mental and emotional well-being are integral to overall health, and that therapy can be a powerful tool for achieving this balance.

What I Offer

A Place to Begin

Individual Therapy

Individual therapy is a space created just for you — where your story is heard, your experiences are honored, and your growth is supported with intention and care.

Couples Therapy

Through couples sessions, we'll explore the patterns, emotions, and experiences shaping your relationship, while building practical tools to help you move forward as a connected unit.

Group Therapy

Group therapy offers a powerful and often deeply healing experience — reminding you that you don't have to navigate life's challenges alone, in a supportive and thoughtfully guided space.

Clinician

Angelique Danza, M.S., LMFT

Thank you for being here! My name is Angelique Danza, Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist based out of Temecula, California. I became a therapist because I've always been drawn to understanding people—their stories, their struggles, and the ways they find strength even in the most difficult seasons of life.

Over the years, both personally and professionally, I've come to deeply appreciate how complex and layered our experiences can be. Life doesn't follow a straight path, and neither does healing. One of the worst feelings is navigating life's struggles without a sense of connection, purpose, and support. My goal is to develop connections and provide support for my clients so navigating life's pivotal moments doesn't feel so isolated.

With over 10 years of providing psychotherapy, I have had the privilege of working with children, adolescents, adults, couples, and in group settings. My experience includes working with clients struggling with grief & loss, anger management, relational conflicts, life transitions, identity loss, anxiety and depression, foster home dynamics, women's mental health, and PC 290 registrants focused on reducing recidivism and supporting public safety.
