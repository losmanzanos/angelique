Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to questions I hear most often. If you don’t see your question answered, please reach out — I’m happy to discuss anything on your mind.

What do therapy sessions cost?

+

Individual therapy sessions are $175 per 50-minute session. Couples therapy is $200 per 50-minute session. Payment is due at the time of service via card or check.

Do you accept insurance?

+

Yes, I accept most major insurance carriers including Aetna, Anthem Blue Cross, Carelon, Cigna/Evernorth, Behavioral Health, and out-of-network benefits. I’m happy to verify your coverage before your first session so there are no surprises.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

I request 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will be charged in full. Life happens — I understand that things come up. Just reach out as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Do you offer sliding scale or payment plans?

+

I’m open to discussing payment arrangements for clients with genuine financial constraints. Please call to discuss your situation. I believe therapy should be accessible, and I’m willing to work with you.

Do you offer virtual (telehealth) sessions?

+

Yes, both in-person and virtual sessions are available. Virtual sessions are conducted securely via HIPAA-compliant video conferencing, so you can receive therapy from the comfort of your home.

How long is the free consultation?

+

The initial consultation is 15 minutes and completely free. This is a chance for us to discuss your needs, answer questions, and determine if we’re a good fit before you commit to ongoing therapy.

How often do I need to come to therapy?

+

Frequency depends on your needs and goals. Some clients benefit from weekly sessions, while others prefer bi-weekly or monthly. We’ll discuss what works best for you during the consultation and can adjust as needed.

What if I don’t have insurance or prefer to pay out-of-pocket?

+

Out-of-pocket rates are $175 for individual sessions and $200 for couples sessions. Many clients choose this option for privacy or because they prefer the simplicity of direct payment. Payment is due at the time of each session.
